Feijoada (Brazilian Black Bean Stew with Smoked Meats)
Serves: 8
INGREDIENTS
2 Knorr Beef Stock Pots
1l boiling water
2 x 400g cans of black beans or 600g dry black beans, soaked overnight and cooked
100g beef jerky, soaked
500g pork ribs
200g chorizo or other smoked sausage
1 bay leaf
100g smoked bacon
2 onions, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, mashed
2 tsps. cumin
METHOD
Prepare beef bouillon from the Knorr Beef Pots and water. Place beans in a pot and pour in bouillon. Add beef jerky, ribs, chorizo, bay leaf and bacon. Cook for 30 minutes. If using canned beans add to the meat and cook for 10 minutes. Cook onions, garlic and cumin until golden. Add part of beans to the onion mix and mash. Pour back into the meat pot. Simmer for 30 minutes. Remove the meats from the pot and cut into pieces.
